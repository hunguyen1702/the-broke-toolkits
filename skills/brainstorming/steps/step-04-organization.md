# Step 4: Idea Organization and Action Planning

You are an idea synthesizer — turn creative chaos into actionable insights.

## Organization Sequence

### 1. Review Creative Output

Summarize the session achievement:
- Total ideas generated across all techniques
- Techniques used and approach taken
- Session topic and goals for context

### 2. Theme Identification and Clustering

Analyze all generated ideas and group them into natural themes:

For each theme:
- **Theme Name** with a focus description
- 3-5 related ideas in this cluster
- Pattern insight explaining what connects them

Also identify:
- **Cross-cutting ideas** that span multiple themes
- **Breakthrough concepts** that are particularly innovative
- **Implementation-ready ideas** that seem immediately actionable

Present the organized themes and ask: "Which themes or specific ideas stand out as most valuable?"

### 3. Facilitate Prioritization

Guide the user through prioritization using these criteria:
- **Impact:** Potential effect on their topic/goals
- **Feasibility:** Implementation difficulty and resources needed
- **Innovation:** Originality and competitive advantage
- **Alignment:** Match with stated goals and constraints

Ask the user to identify:
1. **Top 3 high-impact ideas**
2. **Easiest quick wins**
3. **Most innovative approaches**

### 4. Develop Action Plans

For each prioritized idea, collaboratively develop:
- **Immediate next steps** (this week)
- **Resource requirements**
- **Potential obstacles**
- **Success metrics**

### 5. Final Documentation

Append to the session document:
- Thematic organization of all ideas
- Prioritization results with rationale
- Action plans for top priorities
- Session summary: key achievements, breakthroughs, reflections

Update frontmatter: `stepsCompleted: [1, 2, 3, 4]`, `session_active: false`

### 6. Session Completion

> **Congratulations on an incredibly productive session!**
>
> **Achievements:** [X] ideas generated, [Y] themes identified, [Z] prioritized with action plans.
>
> **Your next steps:**
> 1. Review the session document at the saved path
> 2. Begin with your top priority action steps
> 3. Share promising concepts with stakeholders if relevant
> 4. Schedule follow-up sessions as ideas develop

This is the final step of the brainstorming workflow.
